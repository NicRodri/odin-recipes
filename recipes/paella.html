Paella

Homemade spanish paella recipe with your choice of meat and shrimp. Freezer-friendly and healthy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 200g of fish without spine
  • 200g of uncooked shrimp with the shell intact
  • 200g of sausage or your preferred choice of meat
  • 1 onion
  • ½ red pepper
  • 2 tomatos
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of saffron
  • ½ teaspoon of paprika
  • Add salt/pepper/ and olive oil to taste

Steps

  1. Dice onion, tomatoes, pepper, and celery
  2. Cut the fish into medium strips thick enough that they do not disassemble in the paella
  3. In the paella pan, add olive oil and add the vegetables to sauté in the following order: First the onion until its transparent, then the red pepper, celery, saffron, salt, pepper, garlic powder,sausage, and paprika.
  4. At the point add the shrimp and fry them until they become pink. Once they are pink remove them from the paella and add the tomatoes.
  5. Once the tomatoes are tender, add the rice and mix well with the vegetables
  6. Add the vegetable broth to the paella and mix with the rice. Then add the fish strips cut earlier and place them on top of the rice.
  7. Cover your paella pan( papi usually uses aluminum foil) while the rice is being made
  8. Once the rice is almost finished add back the shrimp
  9. The rice should be finished once there is no longer any liquid and its neither hard nor soft but is firm and slightly chewy
